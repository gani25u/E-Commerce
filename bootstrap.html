Smoothie

smoothie (or smoothie) is a thick, creamy, and nutritious drink made by blending fresh or frozen fruits, vegetables, liquids, and optional add-ins like yogurt, nuts, or protein powder. Unlike juices, smoothies retain all the fiber from their ingredients, making them more filling and wholesome.
